About

Baked By Ant provides New Yorkers with uniquely designed artisanal desserts. Some highlights and specialties include various cookies, cakes, cupcakes, pies, crème brûlées and choux pastries. We strive to use seasonal ingredients as often as possible to ensure that the dish is made using the freshest and most flavorful ingredients. Many of our works can also be modified to be dairy-free, gluten-free, or even vegan.
